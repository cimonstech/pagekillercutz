ABOUT THIS RELEASE

"Page KillerCutz" represents a pivotal shift in the modular synth landscape. Sonic Architect blends raw industrial textures with high-fidelity digital processing to create an auditory experience that feels both ancient and futuristic. Recorded over three months in the heart of Berlin's underground scene, this album captures the kinetic energy of a city that never sleeps.

Produced By Sonic Architect, K. Vane
Recorded At The Grid Studios, Berlin
Label Pulse Records Intl.
Mastered By AudioForge Labs
